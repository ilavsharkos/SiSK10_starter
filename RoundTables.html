Round tables

Round tables are a format for discussions or lectures on a specific topic involving multiple participants, with the goal of encouraging dialogue and the exchange of ideas among speakers on a defined area of science or life. The atmosphere is less formal than at traditional lectures, aiming to encourage participants to express themselves more freely, while also inviting the audience to actively engage in the conversation. We look forward to hearing your contributions at these round tables!
